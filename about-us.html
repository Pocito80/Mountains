WHO ARE WE?
We are a pair of friends who have a shared passion for programming and using technology to make a positive impact on the world. We have always been fascinated by astronomy and the vastness of the universe, and we believe that there is so much more to discover and understand about our place in the cosmos. We are determined to use our skills and knowledge to push the boundaries of what is possible in the field of astronomy and make a meaningful contribution to this exciting and ever-evolving field. We believe that with hard work and dedication, we can help to shape the future of astronomy and make a difference in the way that people think about and engage with the universe. Overall, we are excited about the possibilities that lie ahead and are committed to using our love of programming and our passion for astronomy to change the world for the better.

WHY WE FOUNDED COMPANY?
We founded our company "POLAND MOUNTAIN" in 2016 with the goal of sharing our passion for astronomy and mountain climbing with the world. We have always been fascinated by the mysteries of the universe and the thrill of conquering high peaks, and we knew that we wanted to create a platform through which we could share our knowledge, experiences, and enthusiasm with others. Since starting our company, we have been committed to promoting the beauty and wonder of astronomy and mountain climbing, as well as the importance of environmental conservation and sustainable practices. We have worked tirelessly to organize educational events, expeditions, and community service projects, and we are proud of the impact that we have had so far. We believe that our passion for astronomy and mountain climbing is contagious and we hope to inspire others to pursue their own passions and make a positive impact on the world. We are grateful for the opportunity to share our love of these subjects with the world and we are excited to see what the future holds for our company. So, we are proud to be the founders of "POLAND MOUNTAIN."
